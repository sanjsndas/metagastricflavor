Our Cookies Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2023

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

At Metagastric Flavor Codex, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. Our use of cookies is designed to respect your privacy while providing you with the best possible culinary experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies for different purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Session Cookies Enable basic website functionality and secure areas of the site Session only
Functional Preference Cookies Remember your preferences (language, region, dietary filters) Up to 1 year
Analytics Performance Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our content Up to 2 years
Marketing Targeting Cookies Used to deliver relevant culinary content and offers Up to 1 year

How We Use Cookies on the Codex

Our cookies help us provide the rich, interactive experience that makes the Metagastric Flavor Codex unique:

  • Recipe Personalization: Remember your dietary preferences, allergies, and favorite ingredients to suggest relevant recipes.
  • Culinary Progress Tracking: Track which techniques you've mastered and suggest the next skills to learn.
  • Shopping List Functionality: Save your ingredient lists for meal planning and grocery shopping.
  • Reading Progress: Remember where you left off in our extensive culinary guides.
  • Community Features: Enable you to participate in forums, save favorite recipes, and connect with other culinary enthusiasts.

Note on Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services integrated into our site, such as social media platforms, payment processors, or analytics services. These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Here's how to do it in popular browsers:

  1. Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  2. Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  3. Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  4. Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to access certain features of the Metagastric Flavor Codex.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you use cookies to track my cooking activities?

We only use analytics cookies to understand aggregate patterns in how our content is used, not to track individual cooking activities. This helps us create better recipes and culinary guides.

Are cookies necessary to use the Codex?

Essential cookies are necessary for basic functions like logging in, saving your progress, and maintaining shopping lists. Other cookies enhance your experience but are not strictly necessary.

How often is this policy updated?

We review our cookies policy annually or whenever we introduce new features to the Codex that require different cookie usage. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page shows when we last revised this policy.
